The Mortal Instruments by Cassandra Clare

>

This series is amazing. It follows the life of girl named Clary who finds out that she is a Shadowhunter - a half human half angel hybred whose entire species is dedicated to killing demons. She meets a group of fellow Shadowhunters who together have to save the world. I highly recommend the entire series, there are even spin off's so you don't have to say goodbye to characters when you finish this particular story! Whose your favorite character? I like Clary.

More Info

A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J Maas

>

This is a recent find and new favorite! It's honestly a diamond in the rough. This tells the story of a girl named Feyre who is kidnapped by a fae and forced to live in the fae world. There is an awesome love triangle and amazing character development. I love this story so much. If you like magic, romance, and adventure this book is for you! But be warned, there are only four books in the series but each book is long. Read at your own risk!
